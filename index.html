COBS - Centralized Outdoor Banner System

Connecting Advertisers & Publishers

View on GitHub Download .zip Download .tar.gz

Centralized Outdoor Banner System is a centralized portal to connect outdoor banner space owners (publishers) with people looking to advertise on their space (advertisers) thereby eliminating all middle man in the process and creating transparency in the entire flow.
